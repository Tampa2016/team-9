Our Mission

Our Mission Statement - Help us flatten the world.

WheelShareIt is dedicated to provide people with disabilities with a centralized database of locations where it is wheelchair accessible or inaccessible.

People with disabilities must plan in advance of where they must go to eat, socialize, and be independent. WheelShareIt allows our users to easily find wheelchair accessible locations through our user-friendly search catalog. We also have user-generated polls from our community to prevent people with disabilities to walk into wheelchair inaccessible environments. ...and we'll share it.
